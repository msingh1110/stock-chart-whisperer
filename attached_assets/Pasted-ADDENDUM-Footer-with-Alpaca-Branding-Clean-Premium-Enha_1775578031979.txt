ADDENDUM: Footer with Alpaca Branding (Clean + Premium)

Enhance the footer to include Alpaca branding while improving spacing and visual polish.

Goal:
Create a subtle, professional footer that credits the creator and data source without clutter.

---

1. Content

Display:

"Built by Manmeet Singh • Powered by Alpaca"

- "Manmeet Singh" → clickable (LinkedIn)
- "Alpaca" → clickable (https://alpaca.markets)

---

2. Layout

- Fixed bottom-right
- Horizontal layout:
  [text] • [text]

- Include small Alpaca logo/icon next to "Powered by Alpaca"

Structure:
Built by Manmeet Singh • [Alpaca logo] Powered by Alpaca

---

3. Positioning (improved spacing)

- bottom: 20px → bottom-5
- right: 20px → right-5

---

4. Styling

Container:
- fixed bottom-5 right-5
- px-3 py-1.5
- text-xs
- text-gray-400
- rounded-full
- backdrop-blur-sm
- bg-gray-900/50

Hover:
- hover:text-gray-200
- transition

---

5. Alpaca logo

- Use small inline logo (SVG preferred)
- Height: 12px–14px (h-3 or h-3.5)
- Keep it monochrome or lightly colored to match dark UI

Example:
<img src="/alpaca-logo.svg" className="h-3 inline mx-1 opacity-80" />

---

6. Interaction

- Open links in new tab:
  - target="_blank"
  - rel="noopener noreferrer"

- Only underline links on hover
