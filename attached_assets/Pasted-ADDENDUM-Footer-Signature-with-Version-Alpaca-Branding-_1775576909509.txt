ADDENDUM: Footer Signature with Version + Alpaca Branding

Enhance the footer signature to feel like a polished, production-grade product.

---

1. Content

Display:

"Built by Manmeet Singh • v1.0 • Powered by Alpaca"

- "Manmeet Singh" → clickable (LinkedIn)
- "Powered by Alpaca" → include Alpaca logo + optional link

Links:
- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/manmeetsingh11/
- Alpaca: https://alpaca.markets/

---

2. Placement

- Fixed bottom-right corner
- Always visible across the app

CSS:
- position: fixed
- bottom: 12px
- right: 16px

---

3. Styling (clean + premium)

- Subtle, non-intrusive
- Tailwind example:

text-xs text-gray-400 flex items-center gap-2 hover:text-gray-200 transition

---

4. Alpaca logo

- Use a small inline logo (SVG or PNG)
- Size:
  - height: 12–14px
  - keep it aligned with text baseline

Example:
- place logo before "Alpaca"
- slightly muted opacity (e.g., opacity-70)
