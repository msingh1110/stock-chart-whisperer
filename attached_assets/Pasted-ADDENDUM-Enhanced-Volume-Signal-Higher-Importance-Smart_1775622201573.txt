ADDENDUM: Enhanced Volume Signal (Higher Importance + Smarter Modeling)

Improve the role of volume in the probability engine by:
- slightly increasing its weight
- making the volume signal more expressive (not just binary)
- using it as a confirmation of price movement and conviction

Goal:
Make volume a stronger confirmation signal for breakouts and sell-offs, without overpowering trend and momentum.

---

1. Increase volume weight (moderately)

Update weights:

OLD:
volume_weight = 0.10

NEW:
volume_weight = 0.15

Updated model:

final_score = (
  0.32 * trend_score +
  0.23 * momentum_score +
  0.13 * rsi_score +
  0.15 * volume_score +
  0.10 * news_score +
  0.04 * social_score +
  0.02 * fundamentals_score +
  0.01 * insider_score
)

Principle:
- volume becomes a stronger confirmation layer
- still secondary to trend and momentum

---

2. Upgrade volume signal (from binary → continuous)

Replace simple logic:

OLD:
- high volume + price up → +1
- high volume + price down → -1

NEW: create a continuous volume score

Step 1: compute volume ratio
volume_ratio = current_volume / avg_volume_20d

Step 2: cap extreme values
volume_ratio = min(volume_ratio, 3.0)

Step 3: detect direction
price_change = (price - prev_close) / prev_close

Step 4: compute volume_score

if price_change > 0:
    volume_score = min((volume_ratio - 1) * 1.2, 1.0)
elif price_change < 0:
    volume_score = max(-(volume_ratio - 1) * 1.2, -1.0)
else:
    volume_score = 0

This gives:
- small move + low volume → weak signal
- strong move + high volume → strong signal

---

3. Add breakout amplification (important)

Volume matters most during breakouts.

Add rule:

if price > MA20 and price crosses MA50 upward AND volume_ratio > 1.5:
    volume_score += 0.2

if price < MA20 and breaks below MA50 AND volume_ratio > 1.5:
    volume_score -= 0.2

Clamp to [-1, +1]

---

4. Add low-volume penalty (very important)

Low volume = low conviction

if volume_ratio < 0.8:
    volume_score *= 0.5

---

5. Optional: spike detection

if volume_ratio > 2:
    volume_score *= 1.2

Clamp again to [-1, +1]

---

6. Update explanation logic

Make volume more visible when it matters:

Examples:

Bullish:
"Price is trading above MA20 and MA50, with strong momentum and elevated volume confirming buying conviction."

Bearish:
"Price is below key moving averages, with negative momentum and heavy volume reinforcing selling pressure."

Low conviction:
"Price is above key averages, but muted volume suggests limited conviction behind the move."

Rules:
- mention volume only when:
  - volume_ratio > 1.2 OR < 0.8
- avoid mentioning it when neutral

No new UI elements required.

Volume impact should:
- reflect in probabilities
- reflect in confidence tier
- appear in explanation text

Make volume:
- a stronger confirmation signal
- responsive to real market participation
- especially impactful during breakouts and sell-offs

Without:
